## Dissertation Project  
*Dimensions of Variation in Tikhir, a Language of Eastern Nagaland*

My dissertation investigates **Tikhir**, a Trans-Himalayan language spoken in Eastern Nagaland, India. Working closely with the Tikhir community, I examine:

- The process of dialect differentiation across village networks  
- The evolution of agent-marking dynamics in Tikhir
- 

This project combines community-led documentation with spatial and linguistic analysis to understand how languages interact over time and space. A key aim of the project is not just to generate novel insights on linguistic theory, but also lead to outcomes for the better documentation and descript
